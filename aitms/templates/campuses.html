AITMS CAMPUSES


The study methodology will be carried out through our Campuses at Kochi, Kozhikode, Mavelikkara, Kannur (Coming up) & Thruvanthapuram, which uses the latest techniques of training tools and, increasingly present in everyday and business life.

Our campus is the nerve Centre of AITMS. In addition to containing all the study material, you can use the campus to communicate with your teachers and other students through the use of an internal mail system, chats and forums. This contact will allow you to weave a network of knowledge that goes beyond the simple study of theoretical subjects.

The campus encourages collaborative learning through the implementation of discussion forums, research papers, etc. with the aim of promoting a habit of organized work and innovative study that enhances your entrepreneurial attitude and the skills necessary to succeed.

Seal of Quality


In AITMS we subscribe point by point to the Serious Learning Pedagogy in its attempt to dignify the Learning experience and establish the principles that should serve as a reference to create quality Learning, based on the innovative Integrated Learning.
