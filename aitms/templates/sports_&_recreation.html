SPORTS & RECREATION


At AITMS, we believe that sports and recreational activities are an essential component of a student's holistic development. We offer a variety of sports and recreational activities to our students, including annual sports day, football, cricket, and other sports. Our annual sports day is a highly anticipated event that brings together students from across the institution to participate in a range of sports and recreational activities. The event provides a platform for students to showcase their athletic abilities and engage in friendly competition with their peers. We organize a variety of sports events, including athletics, cricket, football, volleyball, and other outdoor games.

Football is one of the most popular sports among our students. We have well-maintained football grounds where students can practice and play. We organize regular inter-college football matches and tournaments, providing opportunities for students to compete against other colleges and showcase their skills. Cricket is another popular sport among our students. We have a dedicated cricket ground with modern facilities for practicing and playing. We organize inter-college cricket matches and tournaments, enabling students to compete against other colleges and gain valuable experience.

Apart from football and cricket, we also offer other sports and recreational activities to our students. These include basketball, volleyball, badminton, and indoor games such as table tennis, chess, and carom. We have well-equipped sports facilities and trained coaches to support students' training and development.

At AITMS, we believe that sports and recreational activities play a critical role in promoting physical fitness, mental well-being, and social interaction among students.
